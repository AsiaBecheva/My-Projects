How it All Started President and Owner Lynn Farrell started out at Foremost Travel and Tours in 1980 by delivering airline tickets and then becoming a travel agent. After 3 years, she left and started her own travel agency and subsequently merged it with Tom Stelter’s travel agency, Pleasure Travel Unlimited, in 1988. In 2000, things come full circle when the original owners of Foremost Travel and Tours contacted Lynn, letting her know that they’d like to retire and sell the business. So in late 2000, Lynn and Tom bought Foremost Travel and Tours, merging it with their business and operating it under the original name of Foremost Travel & Tours. Sometimes dreams come true and for Lynn to purchase the company where she started out of high school delivering tickets to customers in downtown Chicago is one of them.
